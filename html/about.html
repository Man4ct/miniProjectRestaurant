ABOUT THIS COMPANY

Spotify transformed music listening forever when it launched in 2008.
Discover, manage and share over 80 million tracks, including more than 4
million podcast titles, for free, or upgrade to Spotify Premium to
access exclusive features for music including improved sound quality and
an on-demand, offline, and ad-free music listening experience. Today,
Spotify is the world’s most popular audio streaming subscription service
with 433m users, including 188m subscribers, across 183 markets.

Our mission is to unlock the potential of human creativity—by giving
a million creative artists the opportunity to live off their art and
billions of fans the opportunity to enjoy and be inspired by it.

Our company was created back in 2008 with only 10 staff in the back of
someone's garage. we keep thriving and surviving untill we became big
like this with more than thousand staff and manager to feed.

Our goal is to at least make this company lost lang with no bankruptcy
and always be the best in what it does n still do, which is providing
good music service to the client all over the world, we are here to stay.
